Privacy Policy

Mystic AI Tarot

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Mystic AI Tarot ("we", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

  • Reading history & card collection — stored entirely on your device (iOS UserDefaults / local storage). We do not sync or upload this data to our servers.
  • AI conversation content — when you chat with Luna, your message and the drawn card details are sent to Anthropic's Claude API (or OpenAI's API, depending on your setting) to generate a response. This data is processed by the respective provider under their own privacy policies. We do not store these conversations on our own servers.
  • Subscription status — managed entirely by Apple's StoreKit. We do not store payment or billing information.
  • No account or registration is required to use the app.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, or any personally identifiable information.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or third-party trackers.
  • We do not sell any data to any party, ever.

Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic Claude API — AI response generation. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI API (optional) — AI response generation if selected. Subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit — subscription billing. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

This app is rated 17+ and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Data Retention & Deletion

All local data (history, collection) can be deleted at any time from Settings → Data → Clear All History & Collection. There is no account to delete. Conversation data sent to AI providers is governed by their respective data retention policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
