‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a task?

Tap the "+" button in the Nextday tab to add a new task. You can set a time, add a title, and choose a reminder option.

What are Quick Tasks?

Quick Tasks are customizable one-tap shortcuts for your most common tasks. Create tasks instantly without filling in details every time.

How do Templates work?

Templates allow you to save your Nextday schedule and apply it to future dates (up to 30 days ahead). Perfect for recurring daily routines. This feature requires NextDay Tasks Pro.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Restore Purchases. This will sync your subscription status with your Apple ID.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Manage Subscription, or visit your iPhone Settings ‚Üí Apple ID ‚Üí Subscriptions to cancel anytime.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! All your tasks are stored locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information. No account required.

Will my tasks sync across devices?

Currently, NextDay Tasks stores data locally on your device. iCloud sync is planned for a future update.
