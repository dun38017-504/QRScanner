隐私权政策 (Privacy Policy)

感谢您使用本应用（以下简称“本应用”或“PrivateCamera”）。我们非常重视用户的隐私与数据安全。本隐私政策将帮助您了解我们如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

1. 信息收集 (Information Collection)

本应用不会收集、存储或上传您的任何个人照片、视频或文件。所有拍摄的照片和视频仅保存在您的设备本地的应用专属存储空间中。

The App does not collect, store, or upload any of your personal photos, videos, or files. All captured photos and videos remain stored locally on your device in a secure app-specific folder.

2. 信息使用 (Use of Information)

本应用不会将您的数据用于广告投放或任何第三方用途。如果集成第三方服务（如广告平台），可能会使用设备标识符或匿名数据，仅用于广告展示和统计分析。

We do not use your data for advertising targeting or share it with third parties. If third-party services (such as ad networks) are integrated, they may use device identifiers or anonymous usage data solely for advertising display and analytics.

3. 信息共享 (Information Sharing)

我们不会与任何第三方共享您的照片、视频或个人信息。在法律要求或政府机构请求时，可能依法披露必要的信息。

We do not share your photos, videos, or personal data with any third parties. Information may be disclosed only when required by law or government authorities.

4. 数据安全 (Data Security)

所有文件均加密存储，仅可通过本应用访问。我们将持续优化安全措施，防止数据泄露、滥用或丢失。

All files are stored in encrypted form and accessible only through the App. We continuously improve our security measures to protect against data leaks, misuse, or loss.

5. 儿童隐私 (Children’s Privacy)

本应用不以 13 周岁以下儿童为目标用户，也不会有意收集儿童的任何个人信息。

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. 政策更新 (Policy Updates)

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策，更新后会在应用内或网站上公示。

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be published within the App or on our website.
