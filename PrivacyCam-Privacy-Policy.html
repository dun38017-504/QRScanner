Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

PrivacyCam is designed with a single guiding principle: your media stays on your device, always. We have no servers, no accounts, and no access to your content.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

PrivacyCam does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Photos or videos captured within the App
  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any data uploaded to external servers or cloud services

1.2 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

All media captured by the App is stored exclusively in the App's private sandbox on your device (Documents/PrivateVault/). This directory is:

  • Not accessible by other apps
  • Not backed up to iCloud
  • Not synced to any external service
  • Protected by your vault PIN

We do not have access to this data. We never will.

2. Camera and Microphone Access

The App requires access to your device's camera and microphone solely to capture photos and videos within the App. This access is used exclusively for that purpose. No camera or microphone data is transmitted outside the App or your device.

3. Face ID / Touch ID (Biometric Data)

If you enable Biometric Unlock, the App uses Apple's LocalAuthentication framework to authenticate you via Face ID or Touch ID. The App does not access, store, or transmit your biometric data. Authentication is handled entirely by iOS and Apple's secure enclave.

4. Third-Party Services

PrivacyCam does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking tools. There are no third parties with whom we share your data.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since the App collects no personal data from any user, it is safe for general use, but we recommend parental guidance for users under 13.

6. Data Security

All media files are stored in the App's private sandbox protected by your vault PIN. If you enable the Advanced Shredding (self-destruct) feature, all vault contents are permanently and irreversibly deleted after 5 consecutive failed PIN attempts. We are unable to recover this data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
