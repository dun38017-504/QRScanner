Privacy Policy

Application: NextDay
Effective Date: January 22, 2026
Last Updated: January 22, 2026

Thank you for using NextDay. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our task management application.

In Short: NextDay stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data You Provide

When using NextDay, you may create and manage the following information:

  • Task titles and descriptions
  • Task scheduled times and reminder settings
  • Quick task templates
  • Task templates
  • Task completion history
  • Custom color preferences (Premium users)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

NextDay does not automatically collect any personal information, usage statistics, or analytics data.

2. How We Store Your Data

Local Storage Only: All your data is stored exclusively on your device using iOS local storage (UserDefaults). We never upload, transmit, or sync your data to external servers or cloud services.

This means:

  • ✓ Your data remains private and under your control
  • ✓ No account registration or login required
  • ✓ No data breaches from external servers
  • ✗ Data is not backed up automatically (please use iCloud device backup)

3. Permissions We Request

3.1 Notification Permission

NextDay requests permission to send you local notifications to remind you of scheduled tasks. This permission is:

  • Optional: You can use the app without enabling notifications
  • Local: All notifications are generated and delivered locally by your device
  • User-controlled: You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings

3.2 Other Permissions

NextDay does not request access to:

  • Camera or Photos
  • Location Services
  • Contacts or Calendar
  • Microphone
  • Any other device features

4. Third-Party Services

NextDay does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics or tracking tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No crash reporting services

5. In-App Purchases

NextDay offers premium features through in-app purchases (subscription or one-time purchase):

  • All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not collect or store your payment information
  • Your purchase history is managed by Apple and linked to your Apple ID
  • Premium status is verified locally through Apple's StoreKit framework

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your data to any third parties.

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we have no access to it, there is nothing to share.

7. Children's Privacy

NextDay does not knowingly collect any personal information from users. The app is suitable for all ages (4+) and can be safely used by children under parental guidance.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete individual tasks or clear all history within the app
  • Export: Currently not supported, but all data remains on your device
  • Complete Removal: Uninstalling the app will delete all local data

9. Data Security

Your data security is ensured by:

  • Local-only storage protected by iOS sandbox security
  • No network transmission of personal data
  • No external server vulnerabilities
  • Your device's passcode and biometric protection

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We will notify users of significant changes through app updates.

11. International Users

Since NextDay stores all data locally on your device, no data is transferred internationally. Your data never leaves your device.

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

For California residents: NextDay does not collect personal information as defined by CCPA. All data is stored locally on your device under your control.

13. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

For European users: NextDay complies with GDPR by design:

  • Data Minimization: We collect no personal data
  • Purpose Limitation: All data is used solely for task management
  • Storage Limitation: Data is stored locally under your control
  • Transparency: This policy clearly explains our practices
