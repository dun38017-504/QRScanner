Free & Premium

How many free readings do I get?
Free users receive 1 card draw per day and 3 chat messages per reading session. The daily count resets at midnight.
What do I get with Premium?
Premium unlocks:
  • 10 card draws per day
  • 10 Luna chat messages per session
Choose a Monthly ($6.99/month) or Yearly ($49.99/year) plan in Settings.
How do I upgrade to Premium?
Go to Settings → Unlock Unlimited Luna inside the app, or tap the lock icon that appears when you reach the free limit in chat.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed by Apple, not within the app.
To cancel:
  1. Open iPhone Settings
  2. Tap your Apple ID at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Tap Mystic AI Tarot → Cancel Subscription
Cancel at least 24 hours before your renewal date to avoid being charged.
My subscription isn't showing after I purchased. What do I do?
Go to Settings (in app) → Restore Purchases. This syncs your purchase from the App Store. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used during purchase.

Cards & Collection

Can I recover deleted cards or history?
Your collection and reading history are stored locally on your device and cannot be recovered after deletion or after reinstalling the app. Use Settings → Clear All Data only when absolutely sure.
What does "Cleanse a Card" do?
The Cleanse ritual releases a card back to the universe — it is permanently removed from your collection. This is intentional and cannot be undone. Think of it as a mindful way to refresh your deck's energy.
How are daily draws counted?
Each time you tap Draw My Card or Draw Again, it counts as one draw. The counter resets at midnight local time.

Luna & AI

Who is Luna?
Luna is your AI tarot guide powered by Claude (Anthropic) or GPT-4o (OpenAI), depending on your Settings. She is designed for warm, reflective readings — not fortune-telling. Luna is for entertainment and self-reflection only.
Luna's response is slow or won't load. What should I do?
Luna requires an active internet connection. Please check your Wi-Fi or mobile data. If the issue persists, it may be a temporary outage from the AI provider. Try again in a few minutes or contact us.
Is my chat with Luna private?
Your card and message context is sent to the AI provider (Anthropic or OpenAI) to generate Luna's response. We do not store your conversations on our servers. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Notifications

What notifications does the app send?
If you enable Morning Reading Reminder in Settings, you'll receive one gentle daily notification at the time you choose. We will never send unsolicited marketing notifications.
I enabled reminders but I'm not getting them.
Make sure notifications are allowed in iPhone Settings → Mystic AI Tarot → Notifications. If the toggle is on in the app but you still don't see notifications, try toggling it off and on again inside the app to reschedule.
