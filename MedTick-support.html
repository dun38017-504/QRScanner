❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a medication?

Tap the Medications tab at the bottom, then tap the "+" button to add a new medication. You can set the name, type, dosage note, emoji, color, and reminder schedule.

How do I check in a medication?

On the main screen, tap the check button on any medication card to mark it as taken for today. You can also undo a check-in by tapping it again.

What are Quick Tasks?

Quick Tasks are one-tap shortcuts for frequently taken medications or supplements. Customize them from the Quick Add panel to add check-ins instantly without extra steps.

How do reminders work?

Each medication can have its own reminder schedule. Notifications are generated locally on your device — no internet connection required. Make sure to allow notification permission in iOS Settings.

What's included in MedTick Pro?

Pro features include: full check-in history, statistics, repeat reminders, custom colors, prescription photos, multi-person tracking, and more. Subscribe from the Settings screen.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchases inside the app. This syncs your subscription status with your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions and cancel MedTick Pro anytime. You retain Pro access until the end of the current billing period.

Is my medication data private?

Yes — all your data is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal or health information. No account required.

Can I track medications for multiple people?

Yes! MedTick Pro supports person/profile labels so you can track medications for family members separately on the same device.

Will my data sync across devices?

Currently all data is stored locally on your device. For backup, use your iPhone's iCloud device backup (Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → iCloud Backup).
